Assumptions / Methodology Notes
Period: Mon 2026-05-25 → Sun 2026-05-31 (7-day calendar week). Generated as-of 2026-06-01 morning review.
Net sales source: Dutchie transaction_line_items GAP_BACKFILL (CFO pipeline), totalBeforeTax field, deduplicated by transactionId, Retail type only, isVoid=false, isReturn=false, filtered to local dates 5/25–5/31.
UKG hours: DetailedCalculatedTimeCounters CSV covering 05/25/2026–05/31/2026 (KUR Retailers LLC · KUR/Retail Dispensary). Counters included: Regular, Daily Overtime, Weekly Overtime, _FLSA Overtime, Holiday Worked Closed. PTO/Vacation/DFML/PSL excluded. Non-retail cost centers (Production, Wholesale, Cultivation, Facilities) filtered out.
UKG rates: DetailedRates XLS rate-eff-to = ‘ - ’ (current active rate). Joined to counters by UKG Employee Id; joined to Dutchie leaderboard by employee full name match.
Salaried employees: Employees absent from UKG hourly export but present in Dutchie sales are salaried, not hourly — sales count toward store totals; leaderboard shows SALARIED tag.
W22 policy: Rate column removed from leaderboard per CFO directive. Days Worked, AOV, and Txns Per Hour retained.
Competitor data: Per CFO directive, pricing data from each store’s own website or Dutchie direct embed — no Leafly.
