VII. Methodology Notes
Period: Mon 2026-07-06 → Sun 2026-07-12 (W28). Generated 2026-07-13.
Net sales: Sum of each store's Dutchie transaction_line_items GAP_BACKFILL totalBeforeTax, Retail type, isVoid=false, isReturn=false, deduped by transactionId, per that store's individual dashboard.
